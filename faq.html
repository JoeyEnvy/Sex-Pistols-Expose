Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Doncaster, Mexborough, and the surrounding areas in South Yorkshire.

Are you HETAS registered?

Yes, we are proud to be HETAS registered, ensuring all our installations meet the highest safety and quality standards.

How often should I have my chimney swept?

We recommend having your chimney swept at least once a year, or more frequently if you use your fireplace or stove regularly.

Do you offer warranties on your installations?

Yes, we offer warranties on all our installations. The duration may vary depending on the product and type of installation.

How long does a typical installation take?

Installation times can vary depending on the complexity of the project. A simple stove installation might take a day, while more complex fireplace renovations could take several days.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. Contact us to arrange a consultation.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Get in touch with us for more information.

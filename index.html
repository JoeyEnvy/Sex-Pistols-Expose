Information

Experience the raw energy of punk with Sex Pistols Expose. Our talented musicians deliver an electrifying journey, from snarling vocals to frenetic riffs. Our ultimate tribute to the band that revolutionized music and culture is a high-octane celebration that will leave you breathless.

Approved by all members of the Sex Pistols, the 'Sex Pistols Exposé' are the world's number 1 and only professional tribute, bringing back the anarchic spirit of '77. With Johnny Rotten's snarling persona and Sid Vicious's iconic persona, we bring the sex, the chaos, and the attitude of the original Pistols.

With over 2000 shows between the band members and over 15 years of experience, we've played everywhere from the USA to Japan and across Europe, including legendary venues like the 100 Club. Expect the iconic sound of 'Anarchy in the UK', 'God Save The Queen', and 'Pretty Vacant'.

Occasionally featured in TV and film, the Sex Pistols Expose brings the anarchy of punk wherever we go, creating a raucous celebration of the iconic '77 sound, capturing the legacy of Sid Vicious, Nancy Spungen, and Johnny Rotten.

Our high-energy performances and witty stage banter make Sex Pistols Expose the ultimate punk experience that leaves the crowd shouting for more!
